## Step-by-Step Guide to Designing a Logo with Stockimg.ai

**Step 1: Sign Up or Sign In and Access the Dashboard**

**Step 2: Create a New Project and Set Dimensions**

<CustomImage
    src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/Abdurrahman-Subh/mdx-blog/main/images/stockimg-dashboard.png"
    alt="creating a new project"

Initiate a new project, setting dimensions that suit your logo requirements.

**Step 3: Navigate to the "Logo" Category**

<CustomImage
    src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/Abdurrahman-Subh/mdx-blog/main/images/stockimg-canvas1.png"
    alt="navigating"
/>
On the left side of the interface, find and click on the "Logo" category.

**Step 4: Write Your Prompt or Discover More**
<CustomImage
    src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/Abdurrahman-Subh/mdx-blog/main/images/stockimg-canvas2.png"
    alt="writing prompts"
/>
In the prompt box, express your vision for the logo. Alternatively, click "Discover" for more inspirational suggestions. You can also check our [gallery](https://stockimg.ai/gallery) for more inspritaion! 

Then, click on "Generate" and watch the magic unfold!

**Step 5: Customizing Your Logo**

- *Changing the Size:* Adjust the size of your logo to fit various applications and platforms.

<CustomImage
    src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/Abdurrahman-Subh/mdx-blog/main/images/stockimg-edit-logo1.gif"
    alt="changing the size"
/>

- *Adding Background:* Enhance your logo by adding a background that complements your brand identity. Navigate through the right side. Click on "frame settings" and then change the background color.

<CustomImage
    src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/Abdurrahman-Subh/mdx-blog/main/images/stockimg-edit-logo2.gif"
    alt="changing background"
/>

- *Adding Text:* Incorporate text to convey your brand message effectively. Navigate through the right side. Click on "add text".

<CustomImage
    src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/Abdurrahman-Subh/mdx-blog/main/images/stockimg-add-text.gif"
    alt="adding text"
/>

- *Editing Text:* Edit the text with ease to achieve the desired look and feel. Click on your text once. Then, adjust your setting using the window on the right.

<CustomImage
    src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/Abdurrahman-Subh/mdx-blog/main/images/stockimg-edit-text.gif"
    alt="editing text"
/>

**Step 6: Save and Download**

<CustomImage
    src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/Abdurrahman-Subh/mdx-blog/main/images/stockimg-save-logo.gif"
    alt="saving"
/>

Once satisfied with your logo, save your project and download the final design by clicking "export".
