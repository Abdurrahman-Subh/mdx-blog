A tweet may seem short and sweet, but there's a character limit you need to keep in mind. Currently, Twitter allows up to 280 characters per tweet, but it's often best to keep your tweets concise and to the point for maximum impact.

## Twitter Photo Sizes and Dimensions

<CustomImage
    src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/Abdurrahman-Subh/mdx-blog/main/images/x-photo.png"
    alt="X (Twitter) photo Sizes and Ratios example"
/>

When it comes to sharing photos on Twitter, size matters. Here's a breakdown of the ideal photo dimensions and sizes for your tweets:

- **Single Image Tweet:** Optimal dimensions are 1200 x 675 pixels, with a maximum file size of 5MB.

- **Twitter Card Image:** For website previews, aim for 800 x 320 pixels.

<CustomImage
    src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/Abdurrahman-Subh/mdx-blog/main/images/x-pp.png"
    alt="X (Twitter) profile picture and header example"
/>

- **Twitter Profile Picture:** Your profile picture should be 400 x 400 pixels.

- **Twitter Header Image:** The recommended size for header images is 1500 x 500 pixels.

## Twitter Video Dimensions and Sizes

<CustomImage
    src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/Abdurrahman-Subh/mdx-blog/main/images/x-video.png"
    alt="X (Twitter) video Sizes and Ratios example"
/>


Video content is king on Twitter, but you need to get the dimensions right for optimal viewing. Here's what you need to know:

- **Aspect Ratio:** Aim for a 16:9 aspect ratio for landscape videos and 1:1 for square videos.

- **Video Length:** Twitter videos can be up to 2 minutes and 20 seconds long, but shorter videos tend to perform better.

- **Video File Size:** Keep your videos under 512MB for optimal performance.

- **Supported Formats:** Twitter accepts MP4 and MOV video formats.

## Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

### What is the character limit for a tweet?

Twitter allows up to 280 characters per tweet, including text, hashtags, and mentions.

### How should I format my Twitter profile picture and header image?

Your profile picture should be square (400 x 400 pixels), while the header image should be wider (1500 x 500 pixels) for optimal display.

### What file formats are supported for uploading images and videos to Twitter?

Twitter accepts JPEG, PNG, GIF, MP4, and MOV file formats for images and videos.

### What are the ideal dimensions for images in a tweet?

For a single image tweet, aim for 1200 x 675 pixels for optimal display on Twitter timelines.

### Can I post multiple images in a single tweet?

Yes, you can include up to four images in a single tweet, but ensure each image meets the recommended dimensions.

### What is the maximum video length allowed on Twitter?

Twitter videos can be up to 2 minutes and 20 seconds long, but shorter videos tend to perform better.
