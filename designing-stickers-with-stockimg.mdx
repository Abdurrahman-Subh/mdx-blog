## Step-by-Step Guide to Designing Stickers with Stockimg.ai

**1. Sign Up or Sign In and Access the Dashboard**

**2. Create or Open a Project and Set Dimensions**

<CustomImage
    src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/Abdurrahman-Subh/mdx-blog/main/images/stockimg-dashboard.png"
    alt="stockimg.ai dashboard"
/>

Whether starting fresh or continuing with an existing project, navigate to the "Projects" section. Choose to create a new project or open an existing one. Set the dimensions, selecting from vertical, horizontal, or square, ensuring a perfect fit for your vision.

> Pro Tip: Don't worry! You can change this option later!

**3. Navigate to the "Illustration" Category and Choose the "Sticker" Model**

<CustomImage
    src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/Abdurrahman-Subh/mdx-blog/main/images/stockimg-illustration-category.png"
    alt="finding Illustration category"
/>

On the left side of the interface, find and click on the dedicated "Illustration" category. Then, click on "See all" in the model section. Finally, select the "Sticker" model.

**4. Write Your Prompt or Discover More**

<CustomImage
    src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/Abdurrahman-Subh/mdx-blog/main/images/stockimg-create-sticker.png"
    alt="writing prompts and generating"
/>

Express your vision for your sticker by typing in your prompt. For additional inspiration, click on "Discover" button!

> For this project, we used the prompt: animals, elephant, dog, cat, black cat, white cat, cute animals, comic style, cute design

After writing your prompt choose your desired dimensions, selecting from vertical, horizontal, or square, ensuring a perfect fit for your device.

> Pro Tip: If you are going to print your stickers on a paper, you can use the "vertical" option for better results!

Click on "Generate" to witness the AI-powered magic unfold before your eyes. Stockimg.ai's advanced algorithms bring your vision to life in seconds.
